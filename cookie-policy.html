What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, remember your preferences, or collect usage information. You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Clicking a link does not itself set a cookie on your device from this site.

Managing cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time using:

  • The cookie banner on this site. If you clear your browser cookies, the consent banner will appear again on your next visit and you can choose again.
  • Your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect how sites work.
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Apple Safari
    • Microsoft Edge
  • Google Analytics Opt-out. You can prevent your data from being used by Google Analytics across all sites using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
